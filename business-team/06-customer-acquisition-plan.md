# Customer Acquisition Plan

## Executive Summary

**Goal:** Acquire 100 paying customers within 12 months  
**Target CAC:** <100 SAR per customer  
**Primary Channels:** Digital (80%), Referrals (20%)  
**Geographic Focus:** Riyadh, Jeddah, Dammam (major cities)

---

## Customer Profile Deep Dive

### Primary Persona: "Overwhelmed Owner"
**Name:** Khalid  
**Age:** 35-50  
**Business:** Small retail shop / clinic / restaurant  
**Employees:** 1-5  
**Monthly Revenue:** 15,000-80,000 SAR

**Pain Points:**
- Missing customer messages during busy hours
- Spending 2-3 hours daily answering repetitive questions
- Losing customers to competitors who respond faster
- Can't afford a full-time receptionist
- Not tech-savvy, intimidated by complex software

**Goals:**
- Never miss a customer inquiry
- Free up time for actual business operations
- Look professional and responsive
- Affordable solution that pays for itself

**Information Sources:**
- WhatsApp groups
- Word of mouth from other business owners
- Haraj.com.sa
- Instagram/Facebook business pages
- Local business associations

---
