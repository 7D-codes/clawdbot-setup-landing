## 4. AI Message Data Handling

### 4.1 What We Access
Your AI assistant processes messages between you and your customers. This includes:
- Customer queries and requests
- Your responses and business information shared
- Media files (images, documents) when explicitly enabled

### 4.2 How We Protect Message Data
- **Encryption:** All messages are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
- **Access Control:** Only authorized technical staff can access message data for support purposes
- **Data Minimization:** We only retain message history necessary for AI context (configurable, default 90 days)
- **No Data Selling:** We never sell message data to third parties

### 4.3 AI Training
- Your AI assistant learns from conversations to improve responses
- We do not use your customer conversations to train general AI models
- Training data remains isolated to your specific AI instance
